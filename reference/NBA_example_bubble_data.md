# NBA bubble example data

This data is used to make a bubble plot of the percentage of species of
ecological concern in a group that are affected by a pressure

## Usage

``` r
NBA_example_bubble_data
```

## Format

### `NBA_example_bubble_data`

A data frame with 13 rows and 3 columns:

- taxon_group:

  The names of the taxon groups the species fall into

- pressure:

  The pressures that could impact the taxa

- sub_pressure:

  The specific sub pressures within the pressure categories

- perc_concern_under_press:

  The percentage of species of ecological concern that are impacted by
  the pressure
